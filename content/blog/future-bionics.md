**A Changing Landscape**

For decades, high-quality prosthetics were a luxury. In South Asia, industrial accidents and congenital conditions create a high demand, yet the gap between supply and demand has been massive. ExoGenic is stepping in to fill that gap.

## The Role of 3D Printing
Additive manufacturing has changed the game entirely. What once took weeks of custom fabrication can now be accomplished in days.

- **Speed:** Custom-fitted sockets printed in hours.
- **Cost:** Material costs reduced by up to 90%.
- **Customization:** Perfectly tailored to the individual.
- **Materials:** Carbon-fiber infused PLA offers high strength.

## The Economic Impact
The prosthetics market in South Asia is estimated at over **$2 billion**, yet penetration remains below 5%.

- **Potential Users:** Over 10 million individuals.
- **Current Access:** Less than 500,000 served.
- **Cost Barrier:** Avg cost is 3-5 years of median income.
- **Growth:** 15-20% annual market expansion projected.

## Technology & Challenges
**Convergence of Tech**
- Artificial Intelligence & Pattern Recognition.
- Open-Source Hardware sharing.
- Mobile Connectivity for remote diagnostics.

**Major Challenges**
- Battery life in tropical climates.
- Waterproofing and weather resistance.
- Changing social perceptions about disability.
- Insurance and reimbursement frameworks.
